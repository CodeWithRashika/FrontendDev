Linus Torvalds

The creator of Linux

BIOGRAPHY

As creator of the Linux operating system, Linus Torvalds is a leading supporter of Open Source software. An avid programmer, Torvalds wrote the kernel of the Linux operating system at age 21 from his mother's apartment in Helsinki.Months of determined programming work yielded the beginnings of an operating system known as Linux.
